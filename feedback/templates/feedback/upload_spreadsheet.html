Instructions

Upload your comparison spreadsheet to generate feedback for subcontractors. The system will:

  1. Identify each trade (worksheet) in the spreadsheet
  2. Find columns for each subcontractor
  3. Extract comparison data for each subcontractor
  4. Generate personalized feedback that can be sent via email

The spreadsheet should have one worksheet per trade, with subcontractors arranged in columns.

Email Template

Customize the email template that will be sent to subcontractors. Use the variables below to personalize the email.

Available Variables:
  • {project_name} - The name of the project
  • {trade} - The trade of the subcontractor
  • {first_name} - The first name of the subcontractor contact
  • {company} - The company name of the subcontractor
  • {selection_status} - Whether the subcontractor was selected or not
  • {comparison_table} - The HTML table with comparison data
